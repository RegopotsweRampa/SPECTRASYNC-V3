Cookie Policy

Effective Date: April 25, 2025

At Spectrasync (Pty) Ltd, we use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze our traffic, and understand how you use our site. This policy explains what cookies are, why we use them, and your choices regarding them.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, login sessions, and provide a personalized experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the site to function properly. These cannot be disabled.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand user interactions and improve our website's functionality.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Targeting/Advertising Cookies: Used by third parties to deliver relevant ads.

3. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure website functionality
  • Improve user experience
  • Analyze site usage for performance
  • Deliver targeted marketing (with consent)

4. Your Consent

By continuing to use our website, you agree to our use of cookies. You will also be presented with a cookie banner to manage your preferences.

5. Managing Cookies

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note, disabling cookies may affect your experience on our website.

6. International Compliance

Our policy complies with:

  • South Africa’s POPIA (Protection of Personal Information Act)
  • EU’s General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) (if applicable)
If you are accessing our site from the EU or UK, we will only set non-essential cookies with your explicit consent.
